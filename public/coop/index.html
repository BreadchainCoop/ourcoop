Enter the app

Live cooperative governance: fund balances, project proposals, 100-point member ballots, funding rounds, and fund-withdrawal votes. Browsing is open to everyone; acting needs a member wallet (MetaMask, Ethereum Sepolia) — this page never holds a private key.

Membership & signing

Every transaction is signed by your own browser wallet — this page holds no keys. One member, one vote: your wallet must be added by the coordinator to cast ballots, run funding rounds, or vote on withdrawals. Everyone else can browse everything, live.
